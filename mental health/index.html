What is Mental Health?

Mental health encompasses emotional, psychological, and social well-being, influencing how we think, feel, and act. It affects how we handle stress, relate to others, and make choices. Maintaining good mental health is essential for overall well-being and quality of life, impacting daily functioning and relationships. Mental health is a dynamic state that can fluctuate over time, influenced by various factors such as genetics, environment, and life experiences.
